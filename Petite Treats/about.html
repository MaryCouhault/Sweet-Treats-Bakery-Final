The Petite Treats Bakery Story

Petite Treats Bakery was founded by Christine Chung
and her family, and has been oppurating in Kirwain since 2012.
Inspired by her families passion for making Australian baked goods
for the locals of her home town,
Christine wanted to bring the joy and taste of her childhood for others to enjoy!

Meet Our Baker

Our Baker has an artisan baker who brings flavor and art together for
the beautiful masterpeice which is the fine selection at our menu.

Our Location

We have newly relocated to the corner of Evans Street near Willows Shopping Centre, you can find our humble
bakery nested between the ever bustling streets of Queensland.
Some of our main clients include students and teachers from James Cook University, the Cowboys and Telstra.
